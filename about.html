About the Project

This site meets the assignment’s requirements: a responsive 3–8 column layout, at least two images, navigation between three pages, and JavaScript for theming, animation, and DOM updates.

  • Layout: CSS Grid scales from 3 columns (tablet) to 8 columns (desktop).
  • Images: SVG hero and logo, plus a gallery illustration.
  • Navigation: Home · About · Gallery.
  • JavaScript: Theme toggle, fade-in on scroll, and “shuffle highlights”.

Tip: Try the theme toggle in the header.
